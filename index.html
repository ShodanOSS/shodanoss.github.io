DB Admin. Biochemist. Chef

My background runs through biological science (life), industrial kitchens (chaos), and information systems (order). I follow my curiosity wherever it leads, which skews towards systems and the metabolism of the mind.

My philosophy: Life is by definition the continual endeavour to reverse entropy. Making order from the universe's tendency towards chaos is the fundamental pursuit of all existence; whether physical or digital.

I studied biochemistry to understand how living systems actually work. Working in kitchens taught me that precision, workflow, and timing are what make creativity possible; not inspiration or aspiration alone.

I’m applying that same discipline to technology. I build systems that make it easier for people to think, work, and express themselves.

A good system should feel like a well-run kitchen. Mise-en-place literally buys a lifetime.
